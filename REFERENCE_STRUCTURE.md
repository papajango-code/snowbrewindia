# TheHangoverSG.com Structure - Reference for Snowbrew Replication

## Navigation Bar
- **Style**: Dashed border boxes around each nav item
- **Color**: Green (#2D5016 or similar) with dashed borders
- **Items**: HOME | OUR SERVICES | HOW TO ORDER | CLIENTS AND REVIEWS | PAST EVENTS | FAQ
- **Logo**: Centered at top with tagline "bubble tea live bar"
- **Search**: Toggle search icon on right

## Hero Section
- **Background**: Dark image of bubble tea being poured
- **Text Overlay**: Large white italic text "BUBBLE TEA LIVE STATION"
- **Tagline**: "WE BRING LIFE TO YOUR EVENTS" (gold/tan color)
- **Bottom Banner**: "ONLY AVAILABLE IN SINGAPORE" (gray background)

## About Us Section
- **Title**: "ABOUT US" (centered, italic)
- **Underline**: Gold/tan horizontal line
- **Content**: Paragraph text about commitment to unique, innovative catering

## Our Services Section
- **Multiple Service Cards**: Each service has:
  - Title (e.g., "Bubble Tea Live Station & Catering")
  - Description paragraph
  - Key features/benefits

## Testimonials/Clients Section
- **Carousel**: Slides through client testimonials and past events
- **Navigation**: Previous/Next buttons
- **Cards**: Client logos/images displayed

## Pricing Packages Section
- **Title**: "Choose Your Bubble Tea Live Station Package"
- **Three Tiers**:
  1. Basic Package (100-200 cups)
  2. Premium Package (300-400 cups)
  3. Baller Package (>500 cups)
- **Each Package Shows**:
  - Cup capacity
  - Bullet-point features
  - Pricing area (to be filled)

## Order Form Section
- **Title**: "For Orders"
- **Form Fields**:
  - Name (First/Last)
  - Email
  - Contact Number
  - Company
  - Choice of Live Station/Product (checkboxes)
  - No. of Pax to Serve
  - Event Date and Time
  - Event Location
  - Nature of Event
  - How did you hear about us (checkboxes)
  - Additional comments textarea
  - Submit button

## Footer
- **Links**: Services, Order Form, Bubble Tea Live Station, Coffee Cart, Bottled Bubble Tea, Party Box, Dessert Bowl, etc.
- **Social**: Instagram, WhatsApp links
- **Terms**: Terms and Conditions, Privacy Policy

## Color Scheme (to adapt to BLUE)
- Primary: Dark Blue (instead of green)
- Accents: Gold/tan lines
- Text: White on dark, dark on light
- Borders: Dashed style for nav items
